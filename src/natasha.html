Dr. Vdovichenko Natalia

Head of Medical Research Department & Yoga teacher

Dr. Natalia Vdovichenko was born in physicians family in Kazakhstan. After graduating from Lyceum in Chelyabinsk, Russia she deciced to follow the footsteps of her parents and be a physician. In 2008 she graduated from Chelyabinsk State Medical Academy and continued her education in Gynecology & Obstetrics speciality. After some years of working in her homeland Natalia wanted to develop in her career and started to work since 2011 as a gynecologist in Germany. Since 2011 she starts her yoga practice. During her work by famous cancer specialist in gynecological oncology Prof. A. du Bois she was interested in using of methods of alternative medicine by gynecological cancer patients. She began her education in Ayurvedic medicine for doctors in departement of Integrative Medicine in Kliniken Essen Mitte and University Duisburg-Essen in Essen. In 2015 she decided to partipate in Yoga TTC in Rishikesh where she met Dr. Pandey Ji. Being fond of pranayama & meditation practice she developed her technichs during visit of hymalayan famous spiritual places Badrinath & Kedarnath. She learned vedic astrology and graduated in 2016 from Open International University for complimentary medicine as Doctor of medical Astrology. She practices astrolgy regularly and give private consultations. In 2016 Natalia parctipated as a doctor in practical training in world well-known ayurvedic clinic Arya Vaidya Shala in Kottakal, Kerala to apply theoretical skills in ayurveda to practical routine. Dr. Vdovichenko also cerfied specialist in hypnosis and rebirth therapy from Ther Medius Institute of Germany. Dr. Natalia Vdovichenko works in Germany as a gynecologist, gives yoga classes and guided her research project about efficiency of specific yogic practice by gynecological cancer patients. Since January 2017 she joined Vedansha. Under guiding of Dr. Pandey Ji she created TTC/TC programms and standarts of teaching of Yoga, Astrology, Ayurveda, Yoga Therapy, Kids & Prenatal Yoga, healing Camps, Retreats. My aim as a teacher to guide the student gently in his educational way, not disturb but at the same time show the beautiness & ability of vedic science for someone who wants to change his life in order to connect with himself.
